About the Event

Climate Change Awareness is a project designed to inform and inspire action against global warming. Our goal is to educate students, community members, and anyone concerned about the environment on the causes, effects, and solutions to climate change. Together, we can raise awareness and create meaningful change for future generations.

What to Expect

Your Guide to Climate Action and Education

  • Educational Sessions: Engage with expert-led talks on the science behind global warming and its regional impacts.
  • Community Workshops: Participate in hands-on activities, like building small compost systems or planning tree-planting drives.
  • Interactive Exhibits: Explore data visualizations and models that show the future impacts of rising sea levels and temperature shifts.
